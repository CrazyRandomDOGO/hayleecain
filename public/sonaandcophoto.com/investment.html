I offer Super 8 for couples who want to elevate their wedding memories with something extraordinary, artistic, and deeply meaningful. It captures not just how the day looked, but how it felt—the way your dress moved in the wind, the joy in your first dance, and the subtle, fleeting glances that might otherwise go unnoticed.

Every roll is carefully and intentionally shot by a trusted filmmaker I personally know and work with to bring this medium to life. Once developed in a lab, the footage is hand-edited and color-graded to match the mood and tone of your day—creating an heirloom film as nostalgic and beautiful as your love story.
